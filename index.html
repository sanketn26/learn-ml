This comprehensive 12-week tutorial teaches machine learning and data science fundamentals through realistic SaaS analytics scenarios. You'll learn how to process telemetry data, build predictive models, and extract actionable insights from real-world business metrics.

Learning Style: Conversational, mentor-guided instruction combining concept explanations, hands-on exercises, reflection prompts, and real-world datasets.
